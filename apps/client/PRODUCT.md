# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Grassroots football coaches, players, organizers, and prediction pool participants. They need to manage club rosters, track live scores, handle tournament brackets, run local AI coach analytics, and verify payouts—all offline-first, in low-connectivity situations, directly from their local device wallets.

## Product Purpose
PitchOS is a decentralized, offline-first operating system for grassroots football clubs. It coordinates rosters, tournament formats, scoring events, and prediction pools over P2P replication swarms, ensuring absolute self-sovereignty and wallet-based identity without centralized server reliance.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, high-tech terminal/decentralized. The visual system is dark, precise, cyber-cryptographic, and tech-focused. It values security, speed, tactical precision, and cryptography, conveying the sovereign developer vibe.

## Anti-references
- Generic enterprise SaaS dashboard (avoid cream backgrounds, cookie-cutter cards, corporate/bland templates).
- Overstimulating, flashy sports-betting platforms or overly loud gaming overlays.

## Design Principles
- **Sovereign Transparency**: Expose decentralized states, keys, and P2P connection health with high clarity (like terminal status outputs).
- **Tactical Precision**: Information layout must feel like a strategic playbook—sharp, data-dense, well-proportioned, and high contrast.
- **Offline Resilience**: Design states that respect latency and network absence without feeling broken; show local vs synced state with crisp iconography.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA standard compliance with high-contrast text ratios for outdoor usage (glare under daylight).
- Dark mode optimized with high contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body copy).
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to keep animations minimal or instant.
